1. What are the three main components of the CIA triad?

Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability

2. What is the purpose of hashing in cybersecurity?

Hashing ensures data integrity by generating a fixed-length unique value.

3. What is a zero-day exploit?

A vulnerability that is unknown to vendors and has no patch.

4. What is multi-factor authentication (MFA)?

A security process that requires two or more authentication factors.

5. What is the purpose of a firewall?

To monitor and filter incoming and outgoing network traffic.

6. What is social engineering?

The manipulation of people to obtain confidential information.

7. What is the primary purpose of penetration testing?

To identify and exploit security vulnerabilities.

8. What is the difference between symmetric and asymmetric encryption?

Symmetric uses one key; asymmetric uses a public and private key.
